About Us

Our Legacy of Legal Excellence

Starting off as an independent project, the firm was established by Maître Youssef Barakat in 2011 based on fourteen years of legal experience with Professor Jacque Al-Hakim law firm, and it has grown over ten years to include seven lawyers and legal advisors, who are multilingual and hold both national and international academic qualifications.

"Experience, professionalism, and transparency are the core values of our firm."

We ensure good knowledge of local and international laws, legal systems, procedures, and cultures of several jurisdictions around the world.

Our Expertise

Our firm is dedicated to providing professional legal services with a focus on building lasting relationships with our clients. Whether it's through complex cross-border cases or simple consultations, we approach every matter with confidence and efficiency.
